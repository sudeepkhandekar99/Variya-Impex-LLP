About Us

Variya Impex LLP is one of the leading exporters of fresh fruits and vegetables based out of India.

Our range of products include Alphonso Mangoes, Nashik Pomegranates, Makhana, Nashik red onions, Green chilies, saffron, and cashews.

Our wide array of products are grown in a suitable environment free of harmful chemicals and fertilizers. Further, we not only provide highest quality fruits and vegetables but also at competitive market prices.

At Variya Impex LLP, all our fruits and vegetable are handpicked from all across India to make sure that our customers only get the finest quality products. Further, our packaging team has rigorously worked on creating our product packaging to ensure longevity of our fruits and vegetables. Further, all our packaging is eco-friendly in nature; meaning, we have used renewable materials to reduce environmental degradation.

Our Motto is “to deliver finest Agro Products at affordable prices”. Further, we highly emphasize on making timely delivery to our clients all across the globe. After all, at Variya Impex LLP, our customer satisfaction plays a pivotal role upon which our company is formed. We believe in creating long-lasting business relationships with all our clients.

All our Agro Products are checked for quality by our highly-trained quality management team. We have a bright team of experienced professionals with years of industry-experience. This is the reason why we are able to find the best agro products to export to our clients across the world. As this very team enables us to select the right picking and packaging time as per our client’s requirement.

We also have a packaging and delivery infrastructure in place, which is equipped with the best available technology.
